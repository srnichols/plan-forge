LINKEDIN ARTICLE VERSIONS
=========================

Publishing Instructions:

1. Go to https://www.linkedin.com/article/new/
2. Upload the hero image (listed per post) — 1280x720 px, 16:9 ratio
3. Paste the title into the Title field
4. Copy the body text (below the --- ARTICLE BODY --- line) and paste into the editor
5. LinkedIn preserves bold, italic, links, lists, and blockquotes from rich text

IMPORTANT — After pasting, you MUST manually format:
  - ALL CAPS section headers → Select text → Format as H2 (Heading 2)
  - **Bold text** → LinkedIn usually preserves bold from copy, but verify
  - [IMAGE] markers → Replace each with the actual image from docs/blog/assets/linkedin/
    Click the image icon in the toolbar → Upload from docs/blog/assets/linkedin/

6. Set SEO settings (Manage → Settings):
   - Article URL: use the slug from the title (e.g., "the-80-20-wall")
   - SEO Title: use the article TITLE
   - SEO Description: use the first 2 sentences of the NETWORK TEASER

7. Paste the NETWORK TEASER into "Tell your network what this article is about"
8. Click Publish — then immediately share as a regular post on your feed

TIMING: Publish Tuesday-Thursday, 8-11 AM for peak engagement.
ENGAGEMENT: Respond to every comment within 30 minutes of publishing.
